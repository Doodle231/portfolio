About me



I am originally from Florida. I moved to South Korea in 2014. I have worked as an English teacher. I started out in the Public School system, but for the last few years my wife and I have run our own home school business. It has been a lot of fun but I am ready to transition to a life back home. My degree is in Psychology. I hope to be able to use concepts like color psychology and apply those skills to designing unique and practical designs. I have spent a lot of time working with Vanilla javascript and css without any frameworks. I have tried to focus on basic programming fundamentals up until this point. But I have used frameworks such as React in some projects.

I am willing to relocate to anywhere within the U.S. I have a strong preference for working for a small company. I think working for a small company or startup would be more interesting because in those roles I would probably need to dabble in a lot of different kinds of tech. It would be fun to be able to pitch in on design related problems as well as get involved with other aspects of web dev. If you would like to discuss things further please feel free to contact me.
